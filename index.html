About us

Get to know us and our works
A little more

We are a non-profit organisation

working for the betterment of the poor people in India. We provide various services like free education, food and grains, employment, etc to the poor people in India. Our main focus is to provide all the facilities to the poor and give them employment oppurtunities to help them make a living, this way the poverty in India can be reduced and the employment will be increased. We take donations from the people who willingly wish to help and serve for the betterment of the country. All the donations are taken in the company's official bank account and are only used for the NGO purposes.
